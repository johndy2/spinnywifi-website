About SpinnyWFC

SpinnyWFC is a fan-made project dedicated to restoring online functionality for Nintendo DS and Wii games after the shutdown of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. The goal is to preserve gaming history and keep classic multiplayer experiences alive.

What SpinnyWFC Does

  • Provides replacement servers for DS and Wii online games
  • Offers setup guides for connecting original hardware and emulators
  • Maintains compatibility with a wide range of titles
  • Supports community-driven preservation efforts

Why It Exists

When Nintendo discontinued WFC services, many games lost their online features permanently. SpinnyWFC aims to restore those features so players can continue enjoying the experiences they grew up with. But i'm too young to see what the DS and Wii era was like.

Project Values

  • Preservation: Keeping classic online games playable.
  • Accessibility: Making setup simple for all users.
  • Community: Encouraging collaboration and shared knowledge.
  • Respect: No distribution of copyrighted game files.
